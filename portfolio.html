CHLOE SCREENWRITE

During the course Writing for Film/Broadcast, I worked on writing my very first screen writing play. Over the course of 15 weeks, I learned how to write a logline, develop characters and their own sub-plots, all to enhance the overall plot of my story. Some of the challenges that I faced during this project included ensuring that each character had their own personality that did not take away from the plot.
